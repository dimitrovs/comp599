## Signed social networks *** Links in social networks can be signed: **positive** or **negative** [9]. - Some networks *(eg. Epinions, Slashdot)* allow users to **express link sentiment** - Voting *("thumbs up" / "thumbs down")* can also indicate sentiment towards users - Link sign can be **inferred** from surrounding nodes - Knowing the sign helps **suggest** friends/content - Signs help analyse **influence**
