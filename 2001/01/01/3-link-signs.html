## Signed social networks Links in social networks can be signed: **positive** or **negative** [9]. - Some networks (eg. Epinions, Slashdot) allow users to **express link sentiment** - Voting ("thumbs up" / "thumbs down") can also explicitly state sentiment towards user - Link sign can be **inferred** from surrounding nodes - Knowing the sign helps **suggest** friends/content - Signs help analyse **influence**
