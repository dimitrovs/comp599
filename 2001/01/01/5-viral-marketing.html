## Viral Marketing ## *** - Person-to-person **recommendations** - Effective for advertising of **niche products** - **Excessive recommendations** weaken success rate - Nodes have **influence over few** of their neighbouring nodes - **Smaller** and **"tightly knit"** clusters are more effective for viral marketing
J Leskovec, LA Adamic, BA Huberman. The dynamics of viral marketing, in ACM Transactions on the Web, 2007
